Mill River Visual Analytics

Paradise Pond · Smith College · Northampton, MA

2019 – 2024

This system supports the long-term monitoring of the Mill River upstream and downstream of Paradise Pond. The data tracks macroinvertebrate communities — small aquatic organisms that serve as biological indicators of river health — collected each summer and fall from 2019 to 2024. The goal is to help researchers understand how environmental changes such as conductivity from road salt, water temperature, and stream discharge affect which organisms are present and in what densities.

Researcher: Marney Pratt, Biology Dept. Locations: Upstream & Downstream Seasons: Summer & Fall 238 species · 84 families
